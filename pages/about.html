About Us

Who We Are

Sol Y Salud (Sun & Health) was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to bring the warmth of health education to communities across Colombia who need it most.

Our Story

Our organization began when a group of passionate individuals recognized the gap in health education access between urban and rural Colombia. Inspired by the Colombian spirit of resilience and community, we set out to make a difference.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of healthcare professionals, educators, and volunteers united by our love for Colombia and our commitment to improving lives through education.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach every community with empathy and understanding
  • Education: We believe knowledge is the foundation of health
  • Community: We work alongside communities, not just for them
  • Transparency: We maintain open communication with our supporters
